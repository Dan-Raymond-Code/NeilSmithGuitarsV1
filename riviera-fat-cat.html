Riviera Fat Cat

Bold - flashy - classy. The Riviera Fat Cat is a chambered, double-cut tone machine. While reminiscent of classic instruments, this guitar owns its own voice. This axe covers all the bases from blues to chicken-pickin' to fusion. Its lightweight yet resonant body delivers punchy sustain and glassy clarity.
